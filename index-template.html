About Us

A small Dapto preschool with a very big heart.

At St Luke's, we believe the early years are the most important phase of a child's development. For more than seventy years we've provided a safe, joyful and stimulating place where children learn through exploration, creativity and play.

Our team of passionate, qualified educators nurtures every child's curiosity and confidence, blending warm Christian values with modern early-childhood practice to support the whole child, academically, emotionally, socially and spiritually.

From our calm rooms and natural gardens to the relationships we build with families, every part of St Luke's is designed around one idea: a place you'd feel completely comfortable leaving your child.
