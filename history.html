Since 1953

Seventy years in the same community.

Some of the grandparents who visit us today came here as children. That kind of continuity is rare, and we don't take it for granted.

St Luke's Preschool was first established seventy years ago, in 1953, and has been connected to Dapto Anglican Church ever since. It began in a small weatherboard hall in the heart of Dapto, a humble start for what would become a cornerstone of the community.

As demand grew and the hall reached capacity, the preschool relocated to the minister's house in 1960, a short move up the highway that brought two classrooms and new opportunities for a wider community of children and families.

In 1985 we moved to our current home on Moombara Street, within the Dapto Anglican Church complex, deepening that connection. A demolition and rebuild of the indoor and outdoor environments followed in 1986, carried out with the help of volunteers and leaders from the church, creating a high-quality place for learning.

That work has never really stopped. In recent years we've added updated offices, a sensory bike track, cubby houses, shade sails and an undercover outdoor space. The environment keeps evolving, but the heart of the place stays the same.
