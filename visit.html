Come and see for yourself

Book a visit before you decide anything.

You don't have to be ready to enrol. The best way to know if St Luke's is right for your child is to stand in the room, meet our educators and see the day unfold.

No pressure, ever

A visit tells you what a website never can.

Photos and words can only say so much. In twenty minutes on-site you'll feel the calm, see how children are spoken to, and picture your own child here. Come with every question you have, we'd rather answer ten than have you wonder about one.

What to expect

What a visit looks like.

A time that suits you

We'll find a time during our program so you see St Luke's as it really is, warm, busy and alive with play.

A walk through our world

You'll see the rooms, the gardens, the bike track and the outdoor spaces, and meet the educators who'd care for your child.

Time for your questions

Fees, days, settling in, school readiness, safety, ask anything. You'll leave knowing whether this is the right place for your family.
