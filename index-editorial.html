Where your child begins

Choosing your child's first classroom is a big decision. We don't take that lightly.

St Luke's is a small, deeply rooted preschool in Dapto. Each child here is known by name, by temperament, and by what makes them light up. We believe every child is a unique creation of God, and we build their days around curiosity, kindness and gentle confidence.

Our educators are qualified early childhood teachers who exceed the minimum national requirements, not just meet them. The result is a place that feels calm, safe and genuinely warm, the kind of place you can picture leaving your child and getting on with your day.

Why families choose us

Four things we won't compromise on.

Excellence

We stay close to current early-childhood research and best practice, so the care your child receives is genuinely high quality, every single day.

Relationships

Integrity, empathy and transparency aren't slogans here. We build trusting relationships with children and families, and keep them.

Inclusive

Every family belongs. We work to remove barriers and celebrate culture, so children and parents feel seen, supported and at home.

Passionate

We design rich, hands-on experiences in resourceful environments where children build the skills, and the love of learning, that last a lifetime.

Learning through play

Play is how children make sense of the world.

Behind every puddle jumped in and every block tower built is real learning: language, problem-solving, resilience, friendship. Our play-based curriculum follows each child's interests and gently stretches them, always within the Early Years Learning Framework.

Messy, tactile, sometimes a little bit risky, play is where confidence is built. We make room for it, then we help children put words and ideas to what they've discovered.

Your child's safety

The reassurance every parent is looking for.

You should never have to wonder whether your child is safe. We hold ourselves above the minimum the law requires, and we welcome the stronger national child-safety protections introduced across 2025 and 2026.

Checked, qualified, familiar educators. Secure arrivals and departures. Strict rules on phones and photography. A genuine child-safe culture where children are heard and concerns are acted on.

How we keep your child safe
Working With Children Checks

Every educator, employee and regular visitor is verified, with no exceptions.

Phones & photography controlled

Personal devices are restricted; only secure, service-issued devices capture images, with your consent.

Child Safe Standards, lived daily

A culture where children are listened to and every adult knows how to recognise and report a concern.

A day at St Luke's

Picture your child's day.

A gentle, predictable rhythm helps children feel secure, so they can relax, explore and learn.

8:30am

Soft arrivals

Children are welcomed by name and settle in gently with a favourite activity and a familiar face.

9:15am

Morning gathering

We come together for story, song, prayer and a look at the day ahead, building belonging and language.

10:00am

Outdoor play & discovery

Gardens, sandpit, bike track and big imaginative play, where bodies and friendships grow strong.

11:30am

Creative & intentional learning

Painting, building, early literacy and numeracy, woven into the things each child already loves.

12:30pm

Shared lunch

A calm, social meal together, practising independence, conversation and gratitude.

1:15pm

Rest & quiet time

A gentle wind-down with stories and soft music, time to recharge little bodies and minds.

3:00pm

Afternoon play & home time

More exploration, then warm goodbyes and a story from the day to take home to you.
