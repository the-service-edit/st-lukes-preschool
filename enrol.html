How enrolment works

Four simple steps, no surprises.

Apply to the waiting list

Complete the short form below with your child's details. It takes a couple of minutes.

We confirm within 7 days

You'll receive an email from the office asking for your child's birth certificate and up-to-date immunisation statement.

We offer places by priority

Positions are filled using the Australian Government Priority of Access Guidelines and your application date.

Welcome to St Luke's

When a place is offered, we'll help you settle your child in gently, with an orientation visit before they start.

Good to know

The questions parents ask us most.

What ages do you take?

We're a dedicated preschool for children aged 3 to 5, in the years before they start school.

How are places offered?

From the waiting list, using the Priority of Access Guidelines and your application date. Applying early helps.

What does a day cost?

Fees and available government subsidies are explained by phone or email, so you get accurate, current information for your family.

Can my child visit first?

Yes. Once a place is offered we arrange a gentle orientation visit so the first day feels familiar, not frightening.
